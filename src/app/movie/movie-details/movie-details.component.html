John Wick Parabellum

Action • Crime • Thriller 2019 2h 12min R

After murdering Mafia boss Santino D'Antonio at the Hotel Continental, John Wick is now being chased by members of the High Summit under the U$14 million reward. Now, he needs to join forces with former partners who have helped him in the past while fighting for his survival.
